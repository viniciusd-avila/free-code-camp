Paul Graham

Co-Founder of YC, Hacker News, ViaWeb and ARc
Paul Graham a few years ago

PG has many accomplishments, here is a small list:

  • Co-founded ViaWeb, the first Software As A Service company of the World;
  • Co-founded Y Combinator, the best startup accelerator;
  • Wrote Hackers & Painters, a nice compendium of his essays on many different subjects;
  • Created Hacker News, a news aggregator and forum for the hacker community;
  • He has a PhD in Computer Science from Harvard University;
  • Fun facts, despite his PhD in Computer Science, he has an undergraduate degree in Philosophy and he studied painting for some time in Florence, Italy
